President Diosdado Macapagal High School

President Diosdado Macapagal High School (PDMHS) is a public secondary school in the Philippines, named after the ninth president of the country, Diosdado Macapagal. It serves as an educational institution dedicated to providing quality education to Filipino youth. Known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic student development, the school offers various programs that cater to diverse interests and talents, including academic, technical-vocational, and extracurricular activities.

To provide world-class, superior, high caliber, quality, accessible secondary education attuned with latest innovations capable of transforming the learners to be humane, globally competitive and productive citizens.

The Department of Education was established through the Education Decree of 1863 as the Superior Commission of Primary Instruction under a Chairman. The Education agency underwent many reorganization efforts in the 20th century in order to better define its purpose vis a vis the changing administrations and charters. The present day Department of Education was eventually mandated through Republic Act 9155, otherwise known as the Governance of Basic Education act of 2001 which establishes the mandate of this agency.
