Humanities and Social Sciences

What is HUMSS?

The HUMSS strand stands for Humanities and Social Sciences and is one of the academic tracks offered in the Senior High School (SHS) curriculum under the K-12 program in the Philippines. It is specifically designed for students interested in careers that involve understanding people, society, and culture, as well as developing communication and critical thinking skills.

Choose HUMSS

Focus on Humanities and Social Sciences Core subjects include Philippine Politics and Governance, World Religions and Belief Systems, Creative Writing, Disciplines and Ideas in the Social Sciences, and Trends, Networks, and Critical Thinking in the 21st Century. Promotes the study of society, culture, and human interactions. Strong Emphasis on Communication and Critical Thinking Develops analytical and interpretative skills through subjects like Philosophy, Social Studies, and Literature. Encourages creative expression and persuasive communication.

Builds confidence in public speaking, writing, and interpersonal communication. Prepares students for roles in teaching, counseling, law, media, government, and community development. Enhances empathy, leadership, and problem-solving abilities in dealing with societal challenges. The HUMSS strand is perfect for students who are passionate about social change, enjoy reading and writing, and aspire to make an impact on individuals and communities.
