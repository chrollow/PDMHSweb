What is ABM?

The ABM strand stands for Accountancy, Business, and Management and is one of the academic tracks available in the Senior High School (SHS) curriculum under the K-12 education system in the Philippines. It is tailored for students interested in pursuing careers in the fields of business, entrepreneurship, accountancy, and management.

Choose ABM

Focus on Business and Leadership Core subjects include Principles of Marketing, Business Mathematics, Fundamentals of Accounting, and Organization and Management. Prepares students with skills in managing and leading organizations. Practical and Entrepreneurial Skills Develops abilities in business planning, financial management, and strategic decision-making. Encourages students to conceptualize and execute business ideas through simulations and projects.
