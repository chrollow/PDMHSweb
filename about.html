About Us

School Mission & Vision

To provide world-class, superior, high caliber, quality, accessible secondary education attuned with latest innovations capable of transforming the learners to be humane, globally competitive and productive citizens.

A leading high school that yields world-class learners who are God-fearing, morally upright, socially responsible, intellectually and physically sound and civic-spirited-life skilled individuals.

History

The ground breaking ceremony was led by Honorable former Congressman and now Senator Alan Peter S. Cayetano as project for his constituents in Signal Village, Taguig City and as part of his legislative initiative as he filed House Bill No 5557 to the 12th Congress Second Regular Session and House Bill 6714 to the 12th Congress Third Regular Session entitled: “An Act Establishing a National High School in Barangay Signal Village in Taguig, Metro Manila to be known as the President DiosdadoMacapagal National High School.” Senator Alan Cayetano is considered the main proponent and founder of this school.

Due to overwhelming concern to education and goodness of Senator Alan Peter S. Cayetano, he donated the first four-storey school building project with twenty (20) classrooms. Another additional four-storey building with twelve (12) classrooms was also constructed by the Local Government under Mayor and former Congressman Freddie Tinga.
