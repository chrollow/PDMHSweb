C.A.T

Citizenship Advancement Training (C.A.T.) is a program designed to instill discipline, leadership, and patriotism in students. It emphasizes military training, community service, and civic responsibility, preparing participants to be responsible citizens and future leaders. Students engage in activities such as drills, first aid training, and disaster preparedness exercises, fostering teamwork and respect for authority.

Join!

Journalism

The Journalism Club provides students with opportunities to hone their writing, editing, and reporting skills. Members produce school publications, cover events, and advocate for campus issues, empowering them to express their thoughts effectively. The club nurtures critical thinking, creativity, and ethical journalism, fostering a community of informed and engaged learners.

Join!

Ecosaver

The Eco-Savers Movement focuses on promoting environmental awareness and sustainable practices among students. Members actively participate in recycling programs, tree planting, and waste management campaigns. The movement encourages students to take action against climate change, instilling a sense of responsibility for preserving nature and contributing to a cleaner, greener future.

Join!

Boy Scout

The Boy Scouts of the Philippines (BSP) is an organization that teaches young boys practical skills, leadership, and environmental stewardship. Through camping, hiking, and community service, scouts learn self-reliance, teamwork, and the importance of helping others. The program promotes values such as honesty, loyalty, and responsibility, shaping participants into well-rounded individuals. Join!
